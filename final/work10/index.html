Jeff Dolven considers sentences with incorrect grammar to be meaningful language and uses them to create new language, particularly in the form of poetry. New English Grammar endeavors to construct new languages from flawed sentences, constituting a series of experiments. Through the utilization of graphics, typography, and imagery on paper, it explores new possibilities.
